What makes an ID number valid?

  • The first check we do is to see if the number has exactly 13 digits.

  • We then check the ID number's date and whether it follows the Luhn Algorithm.

Date validity

  • Numbers 1 and 2 indicate the year (78), 3 and 4 indicate the month (03), 5 and 6 indicate the day (13).

  • We check that the month digit is not greater than 12 (and not 0).

  • We then check that the birth day does not exceed the number of days in a month. For example, 31 April is not a valid birth date.
